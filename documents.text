### GET ALL PRODUCTS
GET http://localhost:1600/api/v1/productList HTTP/1.1

### GET SINGLE PRODUCT
GET http://localhost:1600/api/v1/product/:productId HTTP/1.1

### ADD ITEM
POST http://localhost:1600/api/v1/add HTTP/1.1
Content-Type: application/json

### DELETE ITEM
DELETE http://localhost:1600/api/v1/product/:productId HTTP/1.1

### UPDATE ITEM
PUT http://localhost:1600/api/v1/product/:productId HTTP/1.1
Content-Type: application/json

{
  "title": "Updated product title"
}
